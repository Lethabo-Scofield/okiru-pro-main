### How B-BBEE Compliance Works – The Points System

1. There are **7 main pillars** (elements) that give points.
2. Each pillar has a **maximum number of points** (total possible = usually 118–120 points under generic codes, or slightly different under sector codes like Transport QSE).
3. You earn points in each pillar by meeting targets (e.g., % black ownership, % black management, spend on black suppliers, etc.).
4. Your **total points** out of the maximum determine your **B-BBEE level** — the higher the points, the better the level.
5. The **level** gives you a **recognition percentage** (used by companies when scoring you as a supplier — e.g., Level 1 = 135% recognition, meaning you count as 135% of your spend toward their Procurement scorecard).
6. If you fail **sub-minimum requirements** on priority pillars, your level drops by 1 (or more) — even if total points are high.
