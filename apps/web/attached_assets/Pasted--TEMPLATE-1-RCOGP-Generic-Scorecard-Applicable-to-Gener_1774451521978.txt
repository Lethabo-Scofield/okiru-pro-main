=== TEMPLATE 1: RCOGP Generic Scorecard ===
Applicable to: Generic enterprises under the Revised Codes of Good Practice (turnover > R50M)
Pillars: Ownership (25pts) | Management Control (19pts) | Skills Dev (25pts) | Procurement (27pts) | ESD (15pts) | SED (5pts)
Entities to extract:
FINANCIAL:
- TotalRevenue — Total annual turnover/revenue for the measurement period
- NPAT — Net profit after tax
- LeviableAmount — Total leviable payroll (salary bill)
- TMPS — Total measured procurement spend
- FinancialYearEnd — End date of financial year (format: YYYY-MM-DD)
OWNERSHIP:
- ShareholderName — Name of each shareholder/equity holder
- BlackOwnershipPercentage — % equity held by black people (target: ≥25%)
- BlackWomenOwnershipPercentage — % equity held by black women (target: ≥10%)
- ShareholdingPercentage — % total equity held per shareholder
- ShareValue — Monetary value of shares per shareholder
MANAGEMENT CONTROL:
- EmployeeName — Full name of employee
- EmployeeGender — Male/Female
- EmployeeRace — African/Coloured/Indian/White
- EmployeeDesignation — Occupational level (Top/Senior/Professionally Qualified/Skilled/Semi-skilled/Unskilled)
- EmployeeDisabilityStatus — Yes/No (PWD)
SKILLS DEVELOPMENT:
- TrainingProgrammeName — Name/title of training programme or course
- TrainingCost — Cost/spend on training
- LearnerName — Full name of learner enrolled
- LearnerEmploymentStatus — Employed/Unemployed/Learnership
- LearnerRaceStatus — African/Coloured/Indian/White
PROCUREMENT:
- SupplierName — Registered name of supplier
- SupplierBEELevel — B-BBEE level (1–8 or Non-compliant)
- SupplierBlackOwnership — % black owned (target: ≥51%)
- SupplierSpend — Amount spent with supplier during period
ESD:
- ESDBeneficiary — Name of enterprise/supplier receiving ESD support
- ESDContributionType — Grant/Loan/Guarantee/Direct cost/Mentorship
- ESDAmount — Monetary value of ESD contribution
- ESDCategory — Enterprise Development or Supplier Development
SED:
- SEDBeneficiary — Name of SED beneficiary organisation/project
- SEDContributionType — Monetary/In-kind/Time of employees
- SEDAmount — Monetary value of SED contribution
=== TEMPLATE 2: ICT Generic Scorecard ===
Applicable to: Generic enterprises under the ICT Sector Code (turnover > R50M)
SAME AS TEMPLATE 1, PLUS these 2 ICT-specific procurement entities:
- ICTBlackOwnedSpend — ICT procurement spend with ≥51% black-owned ICT suppliers
- ThirdPartyICTSpend — Total spend on 3rd-party ICT products and services
=== TEMPLATE 3: ICT QSE Scorecard ===
Applicable to: Qualifying Small Enterprises under ICT Sector Code (R10M–R50M turnover)
Note: Simplified — Management Control and EE are combined; ESD/SED is a single combined figure
Entities to extract:
FINANCIAL (same as above):
- TotalRevenue, NPAT, LeviableAmount, TMPS, FinancialYearEnd
OWNERSHIP (same as above):
- ShareholderName, BlackOwnershipPercentage, BlackWomenOwnershipPercentage,
  ShareholdingPercentage, ShareValue
MANAGEMENT CONTROL (simplified, no disability field):
- EmployeeName
- EmployeeGender — Male/Female
- EmployeeRace — African/Coloured/Indian/White
- EmployeeDesignation — Top/Senior/Professionally Qualified/Skilled Technical
SKILLS DEVELOPMENT (same as above):
- TrainingProgrammeName, TrainingCost, LearnerName,
  LearnerEmploymentStatus, LearnerRaceStatus
PROCUREMENT (same as above):
- SupplierName, SupplierBEELevel, SupplierBlackOwnership, SupplierSpend
ESD + SED (combined for QSE):
- ESDSEDContribution — Total enterprise, supplier, and socio-economic development 
  contributions combined (target: ≥1% of NPAT for SED + 2% NPAT for SD + 1% NPAT for ED)
The key differences between the 3:

RCOGP Generic → full 7-pillar set, standard procurement targets
ICT Generic → same + 2 extra ICT procurement fields, slightly different point allocations
ICT QSE → trimmed down — no disability tracking, ESD/SED merged into one field, simplified MC
